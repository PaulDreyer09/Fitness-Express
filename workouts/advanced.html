Advanced Gym Routine

So you've decided it's time to lift with the big boys? Thats great, because as you continue to build your muscles you might notice that you are hitting a wall. You try your best with your current workout routine and eat plenty of protein.

All the effort, but it seems to be going slower than before. That means it is time to up your game even more. The workout routine in this section will give you a good idea of what pushing the limit is, and you will reach that too!

This routine introduces working on more than one muscle group in the same session. This allows you to shock your muscles more and more frequently. You will be able to exercise the same muscles more than once a week, and burn through that extra fat that you just can't get rid of.

This routine won't be easy, but you will thank yourself later after you did the extra effort. There aren't any
