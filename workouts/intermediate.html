Intermediate Gym Routine

The key to keep on building on your gains is to keep pushing yourself further than before. If you notice you are getting very used to the exercises and it doesn't feel too challenging anymore then it is time for you to bring up your game. This workout routine is for those who are used to exercise and have a few months of experience. It is time to start pushing yourself and shock your muscles into new growth.

We will be starting on adding more dinamic to our workout. The rep ranges will be dropping lower with each set. Even though the reps are less it will still be more challenging, as you have to increase your weights you are using slightly with each set, so as to push the muscle to the limit even if the reps are less.
